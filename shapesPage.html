About

This was a small week long project from my first year of programming. It is a python applicaion that draws either a triangle, rectangle, or elipse centered on the mouse click with random dimensions. The drawing was all done using PyQt5 and I remember being overwhelmed with the detail of their documentation, but it was a good package to learn how to use. One of the hardest challenges in this project was getting the triangles to draw centered on the mouse click, as there is no built in way to draw the shape with the mouse click as center. To do this, I had to calcualte the center of a randomly generated triangle, and then place the vertecies relative to the mouse click.
